# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This project is dedicated to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for all contributors and community members, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level, nationality, personal appearance, political beliefs, or sexual orientation.

## Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:

- **Be Respectful** — Treat all community members with respect and courtesy
- **Be Collaborative** — Work constructively with others; share knowledge and feedback
- **Be Professional** — Keep discussions on-topic and relevant to project goals
- **Be Honest** — Represent your ideas and give credit where it's due
- **Be Inclusive** — Welcome new contributors and support their growth
- **Be Patient** — Remember that everyone was a beginner once

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are **not tolerated**:

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation of any kind
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory language
- Sexual harassment or unwanted advances
- Posting or sharing private information without consent
- Trolling, spamming, or off-topic disruptions
- Promoting violence, hatred, or discrimination
- Any form of abuse or threats

## Reporting & Enforcement

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

1. **Report privately** — Email `turbo-dev@example.com` with details
2. **Include context** — Share relevant messages, links, or timestamps
3. **Confidential review** — Reports are reviewed confidentially by maintainers
4. **Action** — We will take appropriate corrective action, up to and including removal from the project

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the responsibility and right to:

- Remove comments, commits, or contributions that violate this code
- Temporarily or permanently ban contributors who violate this code
- Escalate severe violations to GitHub trust & safety if needed
