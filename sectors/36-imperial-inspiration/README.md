## ðŸŽ¤ Part 3: Speech Frameworks

### The Imperial Speech Structure

```
1. THE HOOK (30 seconds)
   - Start with a question, quote, or bold statement
   - "What if I told you the impossible is possible?"

2. THE PROBLEM (1-2 minutes)
   - Paint the current reality
   - "We've been settling. Settling for good enough."

3. THE VISION (2-3 minutes)
   - Paint the future possibility
   - "But I see something different. I see an Empire."

4. THE CHALLENGE (1-2 minutes)
   - Call them to action
   - "The question is: Are you ready to build it?"

5. THE CLOSE (30 seconds)
   - End with fire
   - "Let's light the world on fire. Together."
```

### The Three-Act Inspiration

| Act | Purpose | Duration | Key Question |
|-----|---------|----------|--------------|
| **Act I: Was** | Show the old way | 20% | "Where have we been?" |
| **Act II: Is** | Show the opportunity | 50% | "What's possible now?" |
| **Act III: Will Be** | Show the future | 30% | "Who will we become?" |

## ðŸ”¥ Part 4: Lighting Fires (Practical Techniques)

### The 5-Minute Fire Starter

**Use when:** Team needs quick inspiration

```
Minute 1: Share a powerful quote
Minute 2: Tell a story of someone who overcame
Minute 3: Remind them of their past wins
Minute 4: Paint the vision of what's next
Minute 5: Call them to one specific action
```

### The Vision Casting Session

**Use when:** Starting a new project/quarter

```
1. Show where we are (honestly)
2. Show where we're going (boldly)
3. Show why it matters (emotionally)
4. Show their role (specifically)
5. Ask for commitment (directly)
```

### The Recognition Fire

**Use when:** Someone does great work

```
1. Name the specific action
2. Describe the impact it had
3. Connect it to their character
4. Thank them genuinely
5. Share it publicly
```

### The Challenge Ignition

**Use when:** Team is comfortable, needs stretch

```
1. Acknowledge their current excellence
2. Present the bigger challenge
3. Express absolute confidence
4. Offer your full support
5. Set the deadline
```
